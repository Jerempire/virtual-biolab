### The Opportunity

**ERAP2 is the most strongly selected gene in recorded human history** — and it has zero approved drugs.

| Fact | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Selection event | Black Death (1346-1353), +40% survival advantage |
| Evidence | Klunk et al., *Nature* 2022 |
| Disease links | Crohn's, ankylosing spondylitis, cancer immune evasion |
| Drug status | No approved drugs. First selective inhibitors discovered 2022. |
| Our candidate | VAGSAF — AI-designed 6-mer peptide with triple selectivity |

### Why DeSci

Traditional pharma won't touch this because:
1. **No validated target** — ERAP2 has never been drugged
2. **Peptide therapeutics** — perceived as risky (stability, delivery)
3. **Evolutionary biology angle** — not in pharma's playbook
4. **Independent researcher** — no institutional backing

DeSci infrastructure (Bio Protocol + Molecule) solves all four:
- Crowdfunded capital for first wet lab validation
- Open publication of all results (positive or negative)
- Community-driven critique via Beach Science agents
- IP protection via Molecule data rooms if results are positive
